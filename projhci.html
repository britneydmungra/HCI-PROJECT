TaskWhiz


Task management at which one has to be good at to achieve results and discipline has become one of the most pressing issues in a student’s life.Be it the constant procrastination of assignments and projects or the never ending registrations,students tend to lose track of what needs to be done and succumb to their memory only to lose out on important activities. Also, having a proper sleep schedule or efficiently managing time too has proven to be a herculean task thereby reducing total productivity of students,so we have designed a task managing system that helps students manage tasks,track time to achieve productivity.






Concept Video

We wanted to show how why task scheduling is important and how our app can help overcome procastination and help students manage tasks,track time,reminders and so on

Needfinding

The project was thought as the classes have gone online in the covid times it was hard to keep track on the assignments,registrations,class timings, project deadlines which not done in time would be a huge loss,so from the combined eperience of our and our friends data we decided to build an empathy map to gain insights,inputs etc. that helped us in building of the Task scheduling system.
